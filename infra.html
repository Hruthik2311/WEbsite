FACILITIES

Library

We have a well-equipped and well-managed library which is housed in a separate building having three floors on a plinth area of 1000 Sq. metres. The library has a collection of 1.7 lakh volumes of books which includes Text Books, Reference Books & Bound volumes of Journals, Standards etc.

Sports Field

Lords along with Ovals are the two sports field associated with us having a greater area supporting various sports venues for the students.There are also an outdoor basket ball,badminton,tennis facility. Field increases the ability to learn new things and boosts the memory also.

Canteen

Variety of recipes are served having very spacious canteens available near every place in the campus premises.Some of them are Hall 5 Canteen B/L, Cafe Wonder ,Cafe Nescafe,Roy Resteraunt ,Chitrangani ,Chandu Tea Stall ,Techno.We also have food trucks available almost at every hostel

Medica Unit

NIT Durgapur promotes healthy and fit lifestyle of the campus community and focuses on both physical and psychological well-being. The Medical Unit prioritizes intensive care and attentive treatment. One of the most indispensable necessities in the college campus, the Medical Unit of the Institute is served by three resident doctors, three Contractual doctors and other medical staff.

Gym

Cultivating a healthy relationship with fitness for personal well being is vital for every individual and more so with college students.NIT Durgapur has a range of gym facilities which are open to students and staff, offering a wide variety of equipment to help you keep fit on campus and in our halls of residence.

Hostels

We have very spacious hostel facilities with different hostel with all the facilities.NITD provides a comfortable accommodation for its students. It's a second home which ensures that students live out their college lives in a healthy, safe, and conducive environment.
